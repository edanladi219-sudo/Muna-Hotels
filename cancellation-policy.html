Cancellation & Refund Policy

At MunaHotels, we strive to provide the most flexible booking experience possible while maintaining the highest standards of service for all our guests.

Standard Cancellation

Reservations must be cancelled at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled arrival date to avoid a penalty. Cancellations made within the 48-hour window may result in a charge equivalent to the first night's stay.

IMPORTANT REFUND NOTICE:
Please be advised that all approved refunds for cancelled bookings are subject to a 20% DEDUCTION ON REFUND to cover administrative and processing costs.

No-Show Policy

In the event of a no-show, the total amount of the first night's stay will be charged to the guest's account, and the remaining nights of the reservation will be cancelled.

Refund Processing

Once a refund is approved, it may take 7-14 business days for the funds to reflect in your account, depending on your financial institution.
